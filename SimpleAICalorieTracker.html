Privacy Policy for Simple AI Calorie Tracker

This privacy policy is for the app "Simple AI Calorie Tracker" created by Harryo. The app does not send user data outside of what Google and Android handle.

Data Usage and Access Permissions

  • Microphone Access: Required for interval recording. All recordings are stored on your device in the downloads folder.
  • Storage Access: Necessary for saving and loading interval recordings.
  • Internet Access: Used solely for Google AdMob advertisement purposes through Google - Details below.
  • CAMERA Access: Ensures the app remains aware during interval recordings.
  • RECORD_AUDIO Access: Maintains awareness during interval recordings.

AdMob Data Collection

  • Ad Interaction Data: Tracks user interactions with ads (e.g., clicks, impressions).
  • Device Information: Collects device identifiers, IP addresses, and operating system details.
  • Ad Personalization: Uses data for personalized ads based on user behavior and preferences.

Data Storage

All recordings are stored locally on the device in the downloads folder. No further data beyond standard AdMob data is collected.

Conclusion

This policy ensures transparency regarding data collection and usage. For any queries, please contact us.
