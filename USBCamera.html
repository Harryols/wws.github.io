Privacy Policy for USB Camera Last updated: March 22, 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how the USB Camera app ("the App") handles information on your device. CAMERA PERMISSION The App requests access to the camera hardware solely to display the live video feed from a connected USB camera device. The camera feed is shown on screen only and is not recorded, saved, stored, or transmitted in any form. MICROPHONE PERMISSION The App requests access to the microphone solely to capture audio from a connected USB camera device that includes a microphone. Audio is not recorded, saved, stored, or transmitted in any form. DATA COLLECTION The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage data, or any other information. No data leaves your device. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be made available with the App. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. CONTACT If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing.
